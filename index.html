Visit Lima

Lima is a place of converging trends, created by its people and their living culture, where you will find every corner of Peru represented.

Glide through the air

Paragliding is one of the best tourist activities to do in Lima, and it offers the best view of the city.

Try the delicious local cuisine

Lima's cuisine will leave any traveller in taste bud bliss with traditional Peruvian dishes.

Learn or practice surfing

For the adventurous, enroll in surfing classes or practice this great sport on your own.
